Instructions

Welcome mayor. You have been assigned by the Hamster Bureau of Development to manage an underground bunker.

  1. Earn money in the Economy Tab by adjusting taxes. Taxes fund city growth.

  2. As your city grows, you'll see new buildings and hamsters appear!

  3. Curious to what that new building on your screen is? Click on it to find out!

  4. Check on your hamsters in the Hamsters Tab to see if they have enough utilities and houses

  5. Unlock new economic controls as your population grows

  6. Want an in depth overview of your city? Check out the Stats Tab

  7. Find a balance between Housing and Utilities. Too much housing will crowd out all your utilities!
